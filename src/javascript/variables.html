Variables and Data Types

var:Allows reassignment and redecleration. Very flexible and error prone.

let:Block specific. Can only be redeclared outside of the block. what about nested.

const:Also block specific. Can't be reassigned or redeclared. Can only be redeclared in another or nested block.
